WHO AM I

Want an Introduction? You got it.

Hey, I recon you want to know more about me since you scrolled down and haven't closed this tab already. As you might have guessed from the really fancy text above, I go by the name of Rohit Agrawal, currently a third year engineering student at Shiv Nadar University, Greater Noida.
I have been writing code as a hobby for a few years now, just recently decided to make a career out of it. I started with C++ as part of my high school curriculum, but didn't really enjoy it too much. Instead I got into Web Development, which is still going strong till now. From my third semester onwards, I got interested in Machine learning and I have been trying to understand the field more and more ever since.
I went through a brief (2+ years) Designer phase during the end of my high school and the first few semesters of college. I really enjoyed to do 3D modelling and UI/UX Design. Blender3D and Photoshop was my playground of choice. If you'd like to take a look, then check out my Behance profile at the end of the page
